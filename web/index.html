Before we begin

Sketchit is built on a simple principle: the AI learns only from your art, with your full knowledge and consent.

Intellectual Property Agreement

By using Sketchit, you confirm and agree to the following:

  • All images you upload to train the model are your own original work.
  • You are the sole intellectual property owner of all uploaded artwork, or you have explicit written permission from the rights holder to use it for this purpose.
  • You have not uploaded artwork created by other artists, sourced from the internet, or otherwise obtained without full rights and permissions.
  • You understand that this tool is designed to learn from and extend your personal creative style, not to reproduce or appropriate the work of others.
  • The generated outputs are derivatives of your own work and remain subject to your own creative ownership.
  • You will not use this tool to infringe on the intellectual property rights of any third party.
